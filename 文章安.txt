2段熟カレェエー

●流言飛語　りゅうげんひご
False rumors
世間に流れている、根拠のないうわさ。デマ。
Groundless or demagogic rumors spread around town
流言飛語に惑わされず、みんなで力を合わせて頑張ろう。
We have to join hands and do the best we can, without being bewildered by false rumors.

 
●無味乾燥　むみかんそう
To be very boring/To be dry-as-dust
内容におもしろみも味わいもないこと。
Something containing no fascinating or meaningful aspects
昨日のパーティは無味乾燥としていて盛り上がらなかったね。
The party last night was so boring that we didn't enjoy it at all.

 
●大言壮語　たいげんそうご
To be bombastic and boastful
できもしない大きな事を、大いばりで言うこと。
To talk boastfully about things that one will never achieve
アイツは大言壮語の癖があるから本気にしない方がいいよ。
He is always bombastic and boastful. Don't take his words seriously.

 
●侃々諤々　かんかんがくがく
To go at each other tooth and nail
自分の思っていることを遠慮しないで主張すること。
To insist on something without restraint 
侃々諤々と部長に意見を伝えたので、すっきりしたよ。
The section chief and I went at each other tooth and nail. But that was a good opportunity to speak my mind to him. Now, I'm feeling very good.

 
●一刀両断　いっとうりょうだん
To take drastic measures
物事を思い切って、決断して処理することのたとえ。
To make decisions promptly and confidently solve problems
行政改革は一刀両断に行わないと進まない。
We have to take drastic measures to promote the reform of the administrative structure.

 
●行雲流水　こううんりゅうすい
A cloud moving across the sky and a river running through the land
物事に執着しないで自由な気持ちでいるようす。
Being unconfined, not clinging to anything
これからは行雲流水のように暮らしていきたいんです。
I want to live like a cloud moving across the sky and a river running through the land.

 
●外柔内剛　がいじゅうないごう
To be gentle on the outside but tough on the inside
外見はもの柔らかに見えるが、内面はしっかりしていて、芯が強いこと。
A person who is soft and gentle in appearance while at the same time having a tough, strong-minded side
彼女は外柔内剛で奥さんにするには、ぴったりの人だと思います。
She is gentle on the outside but tough on the inside. I'm sure she will make a suitable wife for you.

 
●山紫水明　さんしすいめい
To be famous for scenic beauty
山や川のある自然の光景が美しく清らかなようす。
An area that is famous for its beautiful scenery of mountains and rivers.
山紫水明とは、まさにこの景色のことですね。
The term "Place of scenic beauty" is true to this place.

 
●多情多恨　たじょうたこん
To be full of tears and regrets
物事に感じやすく、恨んだり、悔やんだりする気持ちが多いこと。
To be overly sensitive, and filled with regretful and resentful feelings
多情多恨であるよりも、明るく前向きに生きてみませんか。
You don't have to live a life full of tears and regrets. You should think positive thoughts and live happily.

 
●和気藹々　わきあいあい
To be in happy harmony
和やかな気分が人々の間に満ちあふれているようす。
There is a peaceful and enjoyable atmosphere among the people.
お隣の家族はいつも和気藹々として、楽しそうだわ。
Our next-door neighbors are always in happy harmony. They're enjoying themselves.

 
●自給自足　じきゅうじそく
To be self-sufficient
自分に必要なものをすべて、自分が生産することでまかなうこと。
To provide for oneself sufficiently by producing everything one needs
我が家では野菜はすべて自給自足なんですよ。
My family is self-sufficient in vegetables.

 
●盛者必衰　じょうしゃひっすい
Golden days are destined to come to an end.
現在勢いが盛んな者でも必ず衰えるときがくるものだということ。
Anyone who is intoxicated by success is destined to experience a decline.
バブルの時にベンツを乗り回していた彼が、金策に走ってるのは盛者必衰の見本だね。
He was driving a Mercedes Benz in the bubble era, but now he has difficulty raising money. Anyone's golden days are destined to come to an end.

 
●独立自尊　どくりつじそん
To be independent and self-sufficient
他人の言動に惑わされず、自分自身に誇りをもって自力で生きること。
To live independently, taking pride in oneself and being undisturbed by others
子供達には独立自尊の精神を学ばせたいと思います。
We want our children to learn the spirit of independence and self-sufficiency.

 
●波瀾万丈　はらんばんじょう
be full of ups and downs
物事の進行に変化、起伏が激しいことのたとえ。
There are a lot of drastic changes in the course of events.
是非あなたの波瀾万丈の半生を小説にさせて下さい。
We really want to novelize the part of your life that was full of ups and downs.

 
●純情可憐　じゅんじょうかれん
To be pretty and pure in heart, naive/A pretty ingenue
素直な心で邪心がなく、いたわりたくなるような気持ちを起こさせるようす。
A girl who is so innocent and pure in heart that you want to take care of her
純情可憐な少女の役が私にまわってくるなんて、驚きだわ。
It's amazing that I was cast as a pretty ingenue.

 
●天変地異　てんぺんちい
Extraordinary natural phenomena
自然界に起こる異変のこと。台風、雷雨、地震、大雨など。
Unusual phenomena in the natural world such as typhoons, thunderstorms, earthquakes, or floods
天変地異の繰り返しで、この辺りの地形も変化してきたようだ。
The many extraordinary natural phenomena that we have experienced in this area seem to have changed the topographic features.

 
●先手必勝　せんてひっしょう
Victory goes to the swiftest.
人より先に行動を起こせば、必ず優位に立てるということ。
If you are quicker to take action than others, you will gain an advantage over them.
商品開発は先手必勝だ。
Victory goes to the swiftest. That's how it is in product development.

 
●大胆不敵　だいたんふてき
To be fearless
度胸があって、敵を敵とも思わないようす。
To have a lot of guts and not be afraid of your enemies
彼の大胆不敵な笑みに背筋がぞっとしたよ。
We were frightened to see him with a fearless smile on his lips.

 
●絶体絶命　ぜったいぜつめい
To have one's back against the wall
切り抜ける方法が全く見当たらないほどの窮地に追い込まれた状態のこと。
To be in a critical situation from which there seems to be no way out
会社が絶体絶命の時に助けて頂いたご恩は忘れません。
It was very kind of you to help us when we had our backs against the wall. We won't forget it.

 
●多事多難　たじたなん
To have a lot going on in one's life
事件や困難が多くて、苦労がたえないこと。
To suffer one hardship after another/To be surrounded by accidents and troubles.
彼女は結婚してから多事多難だが、前向きに頑張っている。
She has had a lot going on in her life since she got married, but she is forward-looking and doing everything energetically.

 
●人跡未踏　じんせきみとう
To be trackless/unexplored
人間が今までに一度も足を踏み入れたことがないこと。
Conditions of a region where no human being has ever set foot
世界には人跡未踏の地はまだまだあるんでしょうね。
I think that there are still a lot of unexplored lands in the world.

 
●意味深長　いみしんちょう
To be suggestive
ことばや文章、態度などに含蓄のある意味が隠されているようす。
There is something significant hidden behind someone's attitude and utterances.
昨日の伯父は意味深長で、まだ気になっているんだ。
My uncle said something so suggestive yesterday. I'm still wondering what he really wanted to say.

 
●荒唐無稽　こうとうむけい
To be complete nonsense
言うことに根拠がなく、でたらめなこと。
Someone's sayings are groundless and irresponsible.
彼はいつも荒唐無稽で信用出来ない。
His remarks are always nonsense. We can't believe what he says.

 
●異口同音　いくどうおん
Unanimously
大勢の人が同じことを口をそろえて言うこと。
Many people say the same thing, even if the words are different.
会議では異口同音に社長退任の意見が出された。
At the meeting, the participants unanimously insisted that the president should resign.

 
●時期尚早　じきしょうそう
To be premature
あるものごとをするのに、まだ早すぎること。
Something comes too early. You need more time before you start something.
君が起業するのはまだ時期尚早だろう。
I'm afraid that it's still premature to start your own business.

 
●勧善懲悪　かんぜんちょうあく
To be moralistic
善行を勧め、悪行を懲らしめること。
To encourage good behavior and censure wrongdoing
部長は勧善懲悪を気取っているが、単に独善的なだけだよ。
Our boss is trying to be moralistic, but he is just self-righteous.

 
●暗中模索 あんちゅうもさく
To grope in the dark
手がかりがないので、どうしてよいかわからないまま、いろいろ試してみること。
To try various things without any secure approach to a solution, being puzzled and confused.
事件は犯人の遺留品が何も見つからず、暗中模索の中で捜査が開始された。
The criminal left nothing behind. The police started a search but they are groping in the dark.
 
●難攻不落　なんこうふらく
To be impregnable
攻撃するのに非常に困難で、なかなか攻め落とせないこと。
Something that is difficult to attack/Someplace that is difficult for an opponent to invade
あのチームはセンターバックが難攻不落だが、もしそこを突破出来ればこちらにも勝機がみえてくるぞ。
The center back of that team is so impregnable, but if we break through, we may have a chance of victory.

 
●自由自在　じゆうじざい
In complete control
思いのままできるようす。
To be able to control something freely and easily
このリモコンがあれば、ロボットを自由自在に操ることが出来るんだよ。
With this remote-control device, you will be in complete control of the robot.

 
●風光明媚　ふうこうめいび
To be scenic
自然の景色が清らかで美しいこと。
A place having beautiful and attractive views
日本各地いろいろなところを旅しましたが、こんなに風光明媚なところは初めてです。
I have traveled throughout Japan. This is the most scenic place that I have ever visited.

 
●試行錯誤　しこうさくご
(By) trial and error
いろいろと試みて、失敗を繰り返しながら目標に近づくこと。
To approach success after learning from a lot of failures
この新製品は、試行錯誤を重ねながらやっと出来上がりました。
Through the process of trial and error this new product has at last been developed.

 
●馬耳東風　ばじとうふう
To turn a deaf ear to something
他人の意見や批評を聞き流して気にとめないこと。
To not take somebody's opinions or criticism seriously, and to pay no attention to them
あなたが同じミスを何度も繰り返すのは、私の話を馬耳東風と聞き流しているからです。
You are always deaf to my advice, so it's natural that you keep repeating the same mistakes.

 
●自業自得 じごうじとく 
To lie in the bed one has made

One gets what one deserves. One has to face a crisis that was brought from one's wrong acts.
妻から離婚されても、自業自得だから仕方ないと思っているんだ。
If my wife wants to divorce me, I have it coming. I have to lie in the bed I have made.

 
●切磋琢磨 せっさたくま 
Learning from each other
学問や技芸を練り磨くこと。
To study and work hard to make progress, learning from each other in friendly rivalry
あの二人は、これからも切磋琢磨して伸びていく事だろう。
The two of them will make progress, learning from each other.

 
●奇想天外 きそうてんがい
To be completely novel
普通では思いつかない変わった考え方。
Peculiar ways of thinking that ordinary people would not expect
彼の奇想天外には驚かされる。
We are amazed at his completely novel approach.

 
●空前絶後 くうぜんぜつご
The first and probably the last thing in history
以前にもそれに類する物事がなく、将来にもないと思われること。
Things or happenings that you have never experienced and will never encounter again in your lifetime
あの空前絶後な大プロジェクトに、是非我が社も参画しなければならない。
We have to participate in the great project. It will be the first and probably the last such project in history.

 
●疑心暗鬼 ぎしんあんき
To be gnawed by doubt
疑い出すと何でもない事まで疑わしくなってくるということ。

Since I found out that my husband has had extramarital affairs, I've begun to be gnawed by doubt. Things aren't going smoothly between us.

 
●森羅万象 しんらばんしょう 
All of creation
宇宙間に存在するすべてのもの。
The sum of things existing in the universe

When I fall in love with somebody, all of creation seems to have been prepared for me.

 
●起死回生 きしかいせい

見込みのないと思われたものが再び力を盛り返すこと。

A homer in the bottom of the ninth inning pulled the game out of the fire and that gave us the championship.

 
●用意周到 よういしゅうとう

●危機一髪 ききいっぱつ

●針小棒大 しんしょうぼうだい

●諸行無常 しょぎょうむじょう

●満身創痍 まんしんそうい

体中が傷だらけであること。非難を集中的に浴びたりして、手ひどくやっつけられていること。

●純真無垢 じゅんしんむく

●単刀直入 たんとうちょくにゅう
